User Stories:

MVP:
1. As a user, can I see a list of all articles
2. As a user, can I see a list of my articles 
3. As a user, can I post a comment on a given article

Addtional:
4. As a user, can I delete a comment.
5. As a user, can I serve up a single article by it's id?
6. As a user can I show comments for a given article?
7. As a user can show all the available topics?
8.  as a user can I sort the articles on screen?
